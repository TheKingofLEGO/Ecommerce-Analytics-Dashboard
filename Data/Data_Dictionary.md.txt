# Data Dictionary - E-Commerce Analytics

## Dataset Overview
**Source:** Online Retail Dataset - UCI Machine Learning Repository  
**Description:** Transactional data from a UK-based online retail company  
**Date Range:** 01-Dec-2010 to 09-Dec-2011  
**Total Records (Raw):** 541,909 transactions  
**Total Records (Clean):** ~400,000 transactions (after removing returns and nulls)

---

## Column Definitions

### InvoiceNo
- **Data Type:** VARCHAR(20)
- **Description:** Unique 6-digit invoice number for each transaction
- **Format:** Numeric (e.g., 536365)
- **Special Cases:** 
  - Invoices starting with 'C' indicate cancellations/returns (removed in cleaning)
- **Uniqueness:** Not unique (one invoice can contain multiple products)
- **Null Values:** 0

### StockCode
- **Data Type:** VARCHAR(20)
- **Description:** Unique product identifier assigned to each distinct product
- **Format:** Alphanumeric (e.g., 85123A, 22423)
- **Special Cases:**
  - Codes starting with letters often indicate gift sets or bundles
  - Manual adjustments may have codes like "ADJUST", "BANK CHARGES" (removed in cleaning)
- **Null Values:** 0

### Description
- **Data Type:** VARCHAR(500)
- **Description:** Product name or description
- **Format:** Text string, typically in ALL CAPS
- **Example:** "WHITE HANGING HEART T-LIGHT HOLDER"
- **Special Cases:**
  - Some descriptions are stock adjustments (removed in cleaning)
  - Length varies from 10 to 100+ characters
- **Null Values:** 1,454 in raw data (removed in cleaning)

### Quantity
- **Data Type:** INT
- **Description:** Number of units of the product purchased in the transaction
- **Range:** -80,995 to 80,995 (negatives indicate returns)
- **Typical Range:** 1 to 100
- **Business Logic:**
  - Positive values = Sales
  - Negative values = Returns/Cancellations (removed in cleaning)
- **Null Values:** 0

### InvoiceDate
- **Data Type:** DATETIME
- **Description:** Date and time when the transaction was generated
- **Format:** MM/DD/YYYY HH:MM
- **Example:** 12/1/2010 8:26
- **Range:** 2010-12-01 to 2011-12-09
- **Business Hours:** Primarily 7 AM to 8 PM GMT
- **Null Values:** 0

### UnitPrice
- **Data Type:** DECIMAL(10,2)
- **Description:** Price per unit of product in British Pounds (£)
- **Currency:** GBP (£)
- **Range:** 0.00 to 38,970.00
- **Typical Range:** £0.85 to £25.00
- **Special Cases:**
  - Zero prices indicate adjustments or errors (removed in cleaning)
  - Very high prices (>£1000) are typically bulk items or errors
- **Null Values:** 0

### CustomerID
- **Data Type:** INT
- **Description:** Unique identifier assigned to each customer
- **Format:** 5-digit integer
- **Example:** 17850
- **Unique Customers:** 4,372
- **Null Values:** 135,080 in raw data (24.9% - removed in cleaning)
- **Business Note:** Missing CustomerIDs likely represent guest checkouts

### Country
- **Data Type:** VARCHAR(100)
- **Description:** Name of the country where the customer resides
- **Format:** Full country name (e.g., "United Kingdom", "Germany")
- **Unique Values:** 38 countries
- **Most Common:** United Kingdom (91% of transactions)
- **Other Major Markets:** Germany, France, EIRE (Ireland), Spain
- **Special Cases:**
  - "Unspecified" appears in some records (minimal)
  - "European Community" is used for some EU orders
- **Null Values:** 0

---

## Calculated Columns (Added in Cleaning)

### Revenue
- **Data Type:** DECIMAL(10,2)
- **Calculation:** Quantity × UnitPrice
- **Description:** Total revenue generated from each line item
- **Business Logic:** Only includes positive transactions (sales, not returns)

### InvoiceYear
- **Data Type:** INT
- **Calculation:** YEAR(InvoiceDate)
- **Values:** 2010, 2011
- **Purpose:** Simplifies year-based aggregations

### InvoiceMonth
- **Data Type:** INT
- **Calculation:** MONTH(InvoiceDate)
- **Range:** 1-12
- **Purpose:** Monthly trend analysis

### InvoiceDay
- **Data Type:** INT
- **Calculation:** DAY(InvoiceDate)
- **Range:** 1-31
- **Purpose:** Daily pattern analysis

### InvoiceHour
- **Data Type:** INT
- **Calculation:** DATEPART(HOUR, InvoiceDate)
- **Range:** 0-23
- **Purpose:** Hourly shopping pattern analysis

### DayOfWeek
- **Data Type:** VARCHAR(10)
- **Calculation:** DATENAME(WEEKDAY, InvoiceDate)
- **Values:** Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday
- **Purpose:** Day-of-week trend analysis

## Data Quality Notes

### Issues Addressed in Cleaning

1. **Returns/Cancellations**
   - 8,905 records with negative quantities (returns)
   - Removed to focus on sales analysis
   - Could be analyzed separately for refund analysis

2. **Missing Customer IDs**
   - 135,080 records (24.9%) without CustomerID
   - Removed for customer-focused analysis
   - Represents guest checkouts or data entry errors

3. **Zero/Negative Prices**
   - 1,254 records with UnitPrice ≤ 0
   - Removed as likely data errors or adjustments

4. **Missing Descriptions**
   - 1,454 records without product description
   - Removed for product analysis clarity

5. **Duplicate Invoices**
   - Multiple line items per invoice is normal (not duplicates)
   - One invoice can have 1 to 600+ products

### Final Clean Dataset Stats
- **Records:** ~400,000 valid sales transactions
- **Customers:** 4,372 unique customers
- **Products:** 3,684 unique SKUs
- **Date Range:** 12 months (Dec 2010 - Dec 2011)
- **Revenue:** £9,747,747

---

## Business Definitions

### Transaction
A single line item in an invoice (one product at one price point)

### Order
A complete invoice (can contain multiple products/transactions)

### Customer
A unique individual identified by CustomerID (excludes guest checkouts)

### SKU (Stock Keeping Unit)
A unique product identified by StockCode

### Basket
All items purchased together in a single invoice

### Revenue
Total sales value (Quantity × UnitPrice) excluding returns
